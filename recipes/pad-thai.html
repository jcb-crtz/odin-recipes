Pad Thai

Pad Thai is a popular Thai stir-fried noodle dish, known for its perfect balance of sweet, sour, salty and umami flavors. Made with rice noodles, tamarind paste, fish sauce, tofu or shrimp, eggs and peanuts. It's a vibrant, flavorful street food staple.

Typically garnished with lime and fresh herbs, Pad Thai delivers a delicious mix of chewy noodles, crunchy peanuts, and a tangy, savory sauce. It's a go-to comfort dish that represents the bold and dynamic flavors of Thai cuisine.

  • Shrimp
  • Tamarind paste
  • green onions
  • rice noodles

Steps//

  1. Bring salted water to a boil.
  2. Add herbs and spices to boiling water and let simmer for 2 hours on low
  3. With 15 minutes remaining from the 2 hour timer, add shrimp and rest of ingredients
  4. Add noodles and cook until done.
  5. Serve hot and enjoy your life!
